The Arden Valley Technical Dive

Harriman State Park, NY
Distance55 mi
Ride time1h 30m
StartAstoria, Queens (NYC)
CharacterSharp, technical, elevation-changing corners

Ask local riders where the sharpest pavement near NYC is and Arden Valley Road comes up every time. This route treats the highway and parkway purely as delivery mechanisms: the point is the highly technical, elevation-changing sequence of tight turns that many consider the best pure riding road in Harriman. Shorter than the classic loop, and every mile of the good part works you harder.

The actual road-by-road line, routed by TwoTired's own engine — every curve you see is pavement you'll ride.

How the ride breaks down

The escapeCity streets and standard highway to get clear of NYC.
The transitHigh-speed, multi-lane transit to bypass the suburban sprawl up to Exit 16.
The rewardHighly technical, elevation-changing sharp turns — considered the best pure riding road in Harriman.

Local knowledge

Surface can carry gravel wash after rain — first pass at 7/10ths. Sight lines are short; stay in your lane on blind crests. Combines well with the Seven Lakes loop if you want a full day.
