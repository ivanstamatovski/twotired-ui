TwoTired · Privacy Policy

Effective 29 May 2026.

TwoTired is a motorcycle ride-planning app. We try to do as little with your data as possible, and we never sell it. This page is the truthful version of what gets collected and why.

Who we are

TwoTired is operated by Ivan Stamatovski. Reach us at support@twotired.net for anything privacy-related.

We do not collect: contacts list, advertising identifiers, or anything used for cross-app tracking.

Voice input: when you tap the microphone, what happens to your speech depends on where you're using TwoTired. On the iOS app, recognition runs on your device through Apple's built-in speech APIs — the audio doesn't leave your phone. On the web version, your browser (Chrome, Safari, etc.) handles recognition; that may involve sending audio to the browser maker's servers. We never receive or store the raw audio in either case; we only see the text the recogniser produces.

Who else sees your data

We use a small number of service providers to run the app. Each receives only the data they need to do their job:

  • Supabase (database, authentication, file storage) — stores your account, routes, friendships, and bug reports.
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — receives the text of your ride prompt to turn it into a route plan. Your GPS and email are not sent.
  • Google Places API — receives place names you mention (e.g. "Bear Mountain") to look up coordinates.
  • Resend — sends your sign-in code by email.
  • Vercel — hosts the web version and serves the app.
  • OpenFreeMap — serves the map tiles you see.

We do not share your data with anyone else. We do not sell it. We do not use it for advertising.

How long we keep it

While your account exists, your data stays. When you delete your account (see below), we wipe your rows from our database within 24 hours. Backups roll over within 30 days.

How to delete your data

Open the app menu and tap Delete account. This removes your profile, routes, bug reports, friendships, and shared positions from our servers within 24 hours.

Prefer email? Write to support@twotired.net from the address on your account and we'll do it manually.

Location specifically

Your location is the only sensitive data the app actively collects. We use it for two things:

  • Routing — we send your current coordinates to our routing engine to compute a path from where you are.
  • Sharing — if you've invited a friend, your live position is stored briefly so they can see you on the map. When you stop sharing or close the app, that stops.

We never log your location history persistently for tracking purposes. We never sell location data.

Children

TwoTired is not designed for or directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we change anything material, we'll bump the effective date at the top and let existing users know in-app before the change takes effect.

Contact

Questions, deletion requests, data export, anything else: support@twotired.net.
